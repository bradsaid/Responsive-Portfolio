About Me

My name is Brad Said. I'm from The Woodlands, Texas. In 2005 I graduated from Texas State University with a degree in cultural anthropology. After graduation, I spent two years in the Peace Corps. I lived in rural Paraguay teaching beekeepking.

After finishing two years with the Peace Corps, I attended St. Thomas University in Houston. I graduated from St. Thomas with a MBA and I have been working for Dosey Doe, Inc ever since.
